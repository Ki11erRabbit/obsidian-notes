## What does it mean to nurture?
### What does it mean to Spiritually nurture?
The complementary of motherhood is not priesthood--it's fatherhood
Motherhood is an eternal part of priesthood - John A Woodsoe

"To nurture is to provide love and influence, to care for, support educate, encourage, to protect, and to teach. To nurture is to help someone to grow and develop" - Carol M. Stephens, "Women's Divine Roles Include Nurturing Others"

"The priesthood role of mothers is to influence." - Barbara Morgan-Gardner, "The Priesthood Power of Women"
"the priesthood duty of sisters is to create life, to nurture it, to prepare it for covenants of the Lord." - Barbara Morgan-Gardner

Nurturing is teaching, especially teaching the gospel.
