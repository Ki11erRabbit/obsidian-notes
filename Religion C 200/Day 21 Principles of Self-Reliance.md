"Like two sides of a coin, the temporal and spiritual are inseparable." Dieter F. Uchtdorf "Providing in the Lord's Way"

Self-reliance is the ability, commitment, and effort to provide the spiritual and temporal necessities of lief for self and family. As members become more self-reliant, they are also better at providing from others.

## Principles of Provident Living
1. Husbands and wives should counsel together and use family councils to make financial descisions
2. The foundation of provident living is the law of the tithe.
3. Be an example with the payment of fast offerings. (A fast offering is at leas the cost of the two consecutive meals from which we fast each month).
