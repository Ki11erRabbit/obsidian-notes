"Live the gospel as conspicuously as you can" Jeffrey R. Holland, "A Prayer for the Children"

"Parental hypocrisy can make children cynical and unbelieving of what they are taught in the home." James E. Faust, "The Greatest Challenge in the World - Good Parenting"

"the fundamental purposes for the exercise agency are to love one another and to choose God. ... We have not been blessed with moral agency" David Bednar
